Challah

  • Time: 5 hours
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour
honey
olive oil
eggs
water
yeast
salt		 4 cups
2 tbsp
2 tbsp
4
1/2 cup
1 tsp
1/4 tsp

Step 1: Combine dry ingredients.

Step 2: Separately, combine wet ingredients.

Step 3: Add wet bowl to dry bowl and mix throughly.

Step 4: Knead dough, and add to a greased(with olive oil) bowl. Cover top of bowl with plastic wrap and leave for 2-3 hours in a warm place.

Step 5: Sprinkle a clean, flat surface with flour and dust hands with flour before retrieving the risen dough. Knead more on surface, and divide into 4 equal parts. Roll each part into a long string. Connect one end of each string together and braid.

Step 6: Cover braided challah loosely in plastic wrap. Leave to rise more for 1-2 hours.

Step 7: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Uncover your challah. Crack an egg into a small bowl. Whisk. Cover challah in egg completely.

Step 8: Put Challah in oven for 25 minutes. Let cool and enjoy! Shabbat Shalom!

Step 1: Warm pot on stove. Add butter. Add sliced onions, let caramelize for up to 30 minutes.

Step 2: Add thyme. Add white wine and reduce.

Step 3: Add broth and salt and bring to a boil.
